Case Study: Colgate

Background

With this project, I was tasked with creating an advertisement campaign for a brand of my choosing that would be used on social media, in stores, and in magazines. The brand that I went with was Colgate.

My role in this project consisted of researching Colgate advertisements and then creating sketches of possible layout designs for my advertisements. Using the sketches, I would create the actual advertisement in InDesign.

Results

Process

Creative Brief

The first step in the process involved filling out the Creative Brief. In the Creative Brief, I determined the target audience, the key idea of the campaign, unique selling proposition, call to action, and a short analysis of the brand's competition.

20 ideas

  1. Idea 1
    1. Succinct Expression: Shameless smiles, healthy teeth.
    2. Succinct Expression: Shameless smiles, healthy teeth.
    3. Succinct Expression: Shameless smiles, healthy teeth.
  2. Idea 2
    1. Piercing Insight: Smiles are the first thing that people notice when you start talking.
    2. Brand Connection: Colgate toothpaste helps to prevent tooth decay that can lead to tooth loss.
    3. Succinct Expression: Shameless smiles, healthy teeth.

Sketches

The next step in the process involved creating 30 sketches of possible advertisement layouts for the campaign. I sketched out layouts that would showcase people smiling, a toothbrush and toothpaste, teeth, and dentists.

Digitizing Sketches

After drawing my sketches, I digitized three that I wanted to finalize in Adobe Illustrator. I pulled free stock photos from Google and Freepik and incorporated them into the advertisement. I also utilized the color number Colgate uses for their red color in their own advertisements to keep the brand identity.
